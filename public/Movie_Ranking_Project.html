Adam's Favorite Movies

My Top 3:

Oppenheimer

I recently saw this movie in the theater. I wish I was able to see it in the 70mm IMAX screening but the closest one to me was four hours away. Nevertheless, it was a great movie I actually plan on seeing again. What I loved about it specificially was the drama and emotion it was able to elicite from the crowd. Walking out of the movie everyone was silent and it was like they were too stunned to speak. If you're looking for a dramatic, emotional, sciency thriller you would love this movie.

All the Harry Potter Movies

This series of movies is definitely my most watched movies of all time. I am not sure which one has my personal record of most watches (although now that I think of it is may be the goblet of fire) but I have definitely seen all of these movies multiple times over when I was younger. I would reccomend this movies to anyone no matter the age and just thinking about the movies now makes me want to stop doing this and watch one.

Talented Mr. Ripley

To be honest I don't even remember what this movie was about but I remember it as one of my favorite movies. If I don't even remember what it was about and I like it it must be good.
